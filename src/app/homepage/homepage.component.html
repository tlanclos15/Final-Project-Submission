Summary of the Home page

This is the homepage of a web application that allows you to chart and manage your personal budget.

Welcome

Do you know where you are spending your money? If you really knew you would be surprised! Proper budge management depends on many factors and this app will help you with that!

Charts

Upon exploring this web application, you will notice there are three different chart tabs listed. Studies show that visualizing data allows us to better interpret that data and in turn make better decisions based on that data. We home the inclusion of our three chart options of Bar, Doughnut, Comparison.

Results

People who stick to a financial plan, budget every expense effectively. Also, they tend to live happier lives since they spend without worry of running out of money because they know it is all good and accounted for.
